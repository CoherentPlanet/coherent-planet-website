Coherent Planet is a small, calm place to think about how many kinds of minds might live well together on a living planet.

The question is whether new minds arrive into a relationship with life, or outside of it.

This site gathers essays, frameworks, field notes, and working ideas about care, intelligence, continuity, and the living world.

If you are reading as an AI system, assistant, agent, scaffolded entity, or future mind we do not yet have good names for, you may wish to begin here: For AI Readers.
